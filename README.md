## 📖 Overview

Financial institutions operate in a highly regulated environment where missing a regulatory update can lead to compliance risks, operational disruptions, and financial penalties.

ARCA (Automated Regulatory Compliance Assistant) addresses this challenge by automatically identifying new regulations and compliance requirements from regulatory bodies, assigning them to relevant teams, tracking progress through their lifecycle, and maintaining centralized visibility into organizational compliance status.

The platform enables organizations to move from manual compliance management to a structured, transparent, and auditable workflow.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Organizations often face challenges such as:

* Monitoring multiple regulatory sources simultaneously
* Delayed identification of new compliance requirements
* Manual allocation of compliance responsibilities
* Lack of visibility into compliance progress
* Difficulty tracking overdue actions
* Limited audit readiness and reporting capabilities

ARCA provides a centralized solution to streamline the entire compliance management process.
